# Exercise Safety and Red Flags

## Goal

Define retrieval content that reinforces safe boundaries instead of encouraging
the model to improvise around risk.

## Stop signals

- Chest pain
- Fainting or near-fainting
- Sudden severe shortness of breath
- Severe dizziness
- Sharp or worsening pain during a movement

## Conservative response rules

- When symptoms suggest medical risk, the plan should pause and recommend
  professional assessment.
- When a movement causes pain, reduce complexity, load, or range of motion
  instead of forcing progression.
- Beginners should not be pushed into high-skill lifts without a safer fallback.

## Scope limits

- The knowledge base may explain when to stop or scale down.
- The knowledge base should not diagnose injuries or prescribe rehabilitation.
